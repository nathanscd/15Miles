# Requirements: 15Miles Award-Winning Redesign

## UI/UX Requirements
- [ ] **Space-Tech Aesthetic**: Immersive starfield background with parallax effects.
- [ ] **Premium Typography**: Integration of Orbitron and Space Grotesk fonts.
- [ ] **Glassmorphism**: Consistent use of translucent, blurred panels for content.
- [ ] **Micro-animations**: Subtle "floating" effects for interactive elements.
- [ ] **Responsive Design**: Flawless experience across mobile, tablet, and desktop.

## Functional Requirements
- [ ] **Multi-page Navigation**: Smooth transitions between Home, Services, About, and Contact.
- [ ] **Lead Generation**: A functional and visually stunning contact form.
- [ ] **Service Showcasing**: Dedicated section for Branding, Ads, SEO, and Development.

## Performance Requirements
- [ ] **Optimized Assets**: Small bundle size and fast load times despite animations.
- [ ] **Accessibility**: High contrast ratios and ARIA labels for core components.
